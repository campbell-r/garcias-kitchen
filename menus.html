About Catering

Garcia's Kitchen is known for New Mexican cuisine, but we also offer a wide variety of American classics, such as, roast beef, BBQ chicken, brisket, meat loaf, mashed potatoes, green beans, tossed salad, if you are hungry for it we can make it! There is no event too big or small for Garcia's Kitchen.
Call (505) 242-1199
All catered events are subject to a 15-18% service charge in addition to the per person price. A deposit of up to 50% is required at the time of booking.

How To:

  1. Pick Up - at your favorite Garcia's Kitchen location-give us a call and we will place your large order for you at your most convenient location.
  2. Delivery - we will deliver your food hot & ready to serve to your location.
  3. Delivery & Set Up - we will deliver your food & set up the buffet line for you.
  4. Full Service Catering - trained Garcia’s staff members will set up, serve and clean up after your event; this is our “no worries” option.

All menus include disposable plates, napkins, utensils, your choice of beverage (iced tea, passion fruit tea or lemonade) and buffet set up with two hours of service. China plates, silverware, linen napkins and glassware are available for an additional charge. Our menus can be customized and perfected for your special event. We can make substitutions and revisions to menus as needed and we are always more than happy to accommodate special requests!
Call us at (505) 242-1199 for more information.
